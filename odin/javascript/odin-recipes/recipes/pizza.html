Pizza

Pizza is a delicious dish that originated in Italy. It consists of a flat round base of dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and various other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Add cheese and your choice of toppings.
  5. Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
