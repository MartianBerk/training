Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, meat, and sauce. It's a hearty meal that is perfect for family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Marinara sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onion and minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add ground beef or sausage to the skillet and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in marinara sauce and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  7. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of noodles, then a layer of the ricotta mixture.
  8. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  9. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  12. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
